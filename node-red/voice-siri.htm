Register Siri Shortcut
  • First, create a Siri shortcut, that allows you to speak and send a text message to Node-RED
  • After input keyword (what you want to send to Node-RED) and siri command (what you want to speak), press "Register Siri Shortcut"
  • A layout of Shortcut app open, press "Add to Siri"
  • Now you will have a new shortcut in Shortcut app.
  • To test, speak "Hey siri turn on light one", app will be launched and send "Light 1 on" to Node-RED
