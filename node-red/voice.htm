Speak and send message to Node-RED
  • After speaking, text data will be sent to Node-RED server.
  • By default, app use "admin_url/voice_command" (eg: http://yourdomain.com:1880/voice_command) to post data. So, you need have a "http-in node" with POST method in Node-RED
  • You can also set up your own URL in setting
  • App use 'username' and 'password' of Dashboard for authentication this URL (httpNodeAuth)
